Cabin in the woods

You quietly walk up to the cabin until you're standing just outside the door.
The only sounds you hear are the crickets chirping. Other than that, everything seems quiet.
The immediate area around the cabin gives the impression that someone is currently living here.
Around the side of the cabin, light is still flickering in the only window.

Do you want to knock on the door?
Or do you want to try looking in the window?

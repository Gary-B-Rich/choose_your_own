Pathological

Leaving the main road, you start walking down the dirt path into the deep woods.
Trees have overgrown the sides, and branches scratch your face and arms in the dark.
As you continue on the winding path, the shape of a small building begins to form.
You realize the flickering light is coming from the window of a small cabin.

Do you want to approach the cabin?
Or do you decide to quietly go back to the main road?

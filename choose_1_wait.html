Staying put

You decide to wait in the car and hope someone comes along.
90 minutes pass, and not a single car approaches.
You check your phone again, and there's still no service.
Half of the battery life has already been drained since you broke down.
(Searching for a signal always causes it to die quickly.)
Deciding to save the battery, you power your phone off.
After another 30 minutes pass, you relunctantly decide to start walking.
